Acknowledgement of RPA Safety Requirements

1. I understand that when flying or operating a Remotely Piloted Aircraft (RPA), I am required to comply with all applicable provisions of the Civil Aviation Act and the regulations.
2. More specifically, I understand that I am required to comply with all provisions of Part 101 of the Civil Aviation Safety Regulations 1998, which apply to the operation of RPA generally and in particular circumstances.
3. I further understand that important guidance for the operation of RPA can be found in CASA Advisory Circular (AC) 101-10, Remotely Piloted Aircraft Systems – Operation of Excluded RPA and that I should be familiar with the information contained in the current version of that circular
